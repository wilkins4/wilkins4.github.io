How I work

Small, testable changes with notes someone else can follow.

Document first

I write down the problem, expected behavior, and constraints before jumping into fixes.

Test carefully

I prefer small changes, clear validation steps, and rollback paths over risky rewrites.

Explain clearly

I translate technical work into plain language so another person can understand what changed and why.

Separate experiments

I keep older prototypes and experiments available in the archive without letting them distract from resume-facing proof.
