How I work

Simple habits that make technical work easier to follow.

Document changes

I write down what I changed, why I changed it, and what I checked afterward.

Test small

I test small before changing large so the next step is easier to understand.

Avoid guesswork

I prefer clear notes, observable results, and repeatable steps over vague fixes.

Explain clearly

I try to explain technical work so someone else can follow it later.
