Lab notes

Proof of learning, written clearly.

This page is the organizing frame for security, networking, and forensics notes. The goal is to turn coursework and homelab work into short, readable writeups with tools, steps, findings, and lessons learned.

Lab writeup template

  • Objective: What was I trying to prove or learn?
  • Environment: OS, tools, network, and constraints.
  • Procedure: The steps I took.
  • Findings: What I observed.
  • Resolution: What changed or what I concluded.
  • Lessons learned: What I would do differently next time.
